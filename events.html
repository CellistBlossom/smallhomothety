HKSC Events

Periodically, the Hollow Knight Speedrunning community hosts community-led events on our community Twitch channel. Usually, these events take the form of showcases or tournaments.

Hollow Knight was released on February 24th, 2017. Since the fourth anniversary, we have held annual celebration events! Keep watch on our channel when the time comes.

Events may take place over the course of a single day, a few days, or several weeks or months, depending on the projected length of the event. Celebrations and showcases are usually day-long or week-long, and tournaments tend to land in the weeks-to-months range, though this can vary.

For tournaments specific to the Randomizer section of the community, see the randomizer tournaments page.
