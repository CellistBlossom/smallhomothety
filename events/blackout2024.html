Blackout True Ending Tournament 2024

For more information on randomizer tournaments like this one, see the randomizer tournaments page.


The 2024 Blackout TE tournament has begun! Blackout True Ending is a format played with a 5x5 bingo board. The tournament will be played 2v2; players choose their teammate before the tournament and will advance through the bracket as a team. The first team to mark all 25 goals on the bingo board and have both players beat the Radiance wins the match! For more information on randomizer formats, see the formats page.

The tournament format begins with a group stage, and the top teams from each group will advance to a double-elimination bracket. The 25 teams have been sorted into 5 groups of 5, and each team will play each of the other teams in their own group twice.

Like the previous 2023 All Majors Tournament, this tournament is allowing gimmicks! Gimmicks are items that will be added to the randomized pool beyond the default settings to help spice up each match. Both teams will offer the other team a list of three potential gimmicks, and one will be picked from each list. Gimmicks are optional for the first round, but every round in the rest of the tournament will have a minimum of two gimmicks.

The list of gimmicks allowed for the 2024 Blackout TE tournament are:

Gimmicks that randomize additional checks: Grimmkin Flames, Whispering Roots, Lore Tablets, Randomized Focus

Settings that alter logic with skips: Precise Movement, Background and Enemy Pogos, Infection Skips, No Swim/No Tear

Settings that alter how rando functions: Ghost Essence, Lemm/Junk Shops, Colo3 (the Glory of Being a Fool mod)

Gimmicks voted upon by rando community: Replace the middle goal of the board with "obtain 46 grubs"; Map-Area randomizer; Access Randomizer (chains + unique keys + respect), Split Claw, Randomized Levers, More Doors (all doors, no keys), the Real Journal Rando (no notes, no bonus entries), Cursed Nail.
