Blackout True Ending Randomizer Tournament 2023

For more information on randomizer tournaments like this one, see the randomizer tournaments page.


This tournament was a 2v2 double-elimination Blackout TE tournament that ran from March 3rd, 2023 to March 29th. It was restreamed on the HollowKnightCommunity Twitch channel and is now highlighted there.

Blackout is a randomizer racing format played with a bingo board. The first team to mark all 25 goals on the board and have every member kill Radiance wins. Only one person in a team needs to complete a goal for the team to mark it. However, both players on the team must beat Radiance; once both have beaten Radiance, GG can be called.

See this document for the goal rules in this tournament (credit Adyn). Also see the Blackout 2023 Tournament Rules and general Randomizer rules for more information.
