Holloween 2025

Art by burkicle

Holloween was a halloween-themed event featuring spooky Hollow Knight speedrunning categories and randomizer races. The event took place on October 4th, 2025 beginning at 2pm UTC (10am EDT) and lasting for around 18 hours! It was streamed on the speedrunning and rando community's Twitch Channel.
