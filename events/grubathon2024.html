Grub-a-Thon 2024

Grub-a-Thon is an event all about saving grubs! The event will take place on April 6th, 2024, and will start at 3:30PM UTC (11:30AM EDT). We've decided that our squishy little buddies deserve a day of their own, so we'll be running speedrun categories and doing randomizer races where saving grubs is a main feature. Mee maw maw!

The schedule for the event is below. Please keep in mind that this schedule is subject to change. Credit to dragon_nyx for the cool png version!
