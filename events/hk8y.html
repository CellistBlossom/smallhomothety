Hollow Knight 8th Anniversary Celebration

HK8Y is the Hollow Knight 8th anniversary event! The event was spread out over the course of three days: main day, meme day, and rando day. The main event was on Saturday, February 22th, 2025 starting at 3pm UTC (10am EST).

The schedule for the main event is below. Credit to dragon_nyx for the cool png version!
