All Whispering Roots Tournament 2022

This tournament was a 4-way knockout speedrunning tournament for the All Whispering Roots No Major Glitches category. The tournament began with a qualifier phase where runners were given a week to get the fastest time that they could. The top 4 players advanced to the knockout phase, held on March 20, 2022.

The tournament consisted of three matches. Each round, the player with the slowest time would be knocked out, until only one player was left. It was restreamed on the community Twitch channel but was unfortunately not highlighted.
