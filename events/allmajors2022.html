All Majors Randomizer Tournament 2022

For more information on randomizer tournaments like this one, see the randomizer tournaments page.


This tournament was a 2v2 double-elimination All Majors tournament that ran from June 1st, 2022 to July 24th, 2022. It was restreamed on the HollowKnightCommunity Twitch channel and is now highlighted there.

All Majors is a semi-spoiler randomizer racing format played with the MajorItemsByArea mod. Runners have a tracker onscreen listing the number of major items in each area, but they don't know the exact locations of the items, or which major items were placed in what area. Each team is required to get every item on the tracker, and once the counter reaches zero, defeat the Radiance. The first team to have both players finish Radiance wins!
