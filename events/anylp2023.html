Latest Patch Any% NMG Tournament 2023

This tournament was a speedrunning tournament for the 1.4.3.2+ Any% No Major Glitches category, which ran from March 13th 2023 to May 12th 2023. It was restreamed on the HollowKnightCommunity Twitch channel and is now highlighted there. This event immediately followed the 1.2.2.1 Any% NMG Tournament 2022.

The tournament consisted of two phases. The first part of the tournament was a round-robin tournament to determine the top 16 players, who would then progress to a single-elimination bracket in the second phase.
