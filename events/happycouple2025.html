Happy Couple Fundraiser 2025

The speedrunning community's fifth annual pride month fundraiser starts on June 1st! This month, we're raising money for the ACLU. Various members of the community will be doing charity streams all month long, so keep your eye out for Twitch streams this month!

By donating, you can support efforts to defend the rights of the LGBTQ community, and as a bonus, enter a raffle to win various prizes made by speedrunning community members. A small selection of planned streams for this month is listed below:

June 1st at 2pm EDT: Tsira is doing a Happy Couple rando!

June 7th beginning at 10am EDT (and lasting until past midnight!): Grub-a-Thon!

???: Fireb0rn, Mossbag, and PhantasticalPhiasco also plan to do charity streams!

...and many more TBA during the month!

A total of 13 prizes will be raffled off for the fundraiser, including plushies, art commissions, and more. You can get tickets for the raffle by donating.

Getting Involved

This isn't just limited to big streamers or the most central members of the speedrunning community. If you want, you can host a charity stream for the fundraiser too!

You'll want to use one of the following overlays for your stream setup, so that people can easily find where to donate:

Stream overlay with top layout

Stream overlay with bottom layout

After you've picked one of these, add it as a browser source in OBS, and set the size to 1920x1080.

If you use a stream bot in your chat such as Streamelements or Nightbot, you're encouraged to add a command for the fundraiser, too. Here are some example commands:

We are raising money for the ACLU this Pride Month, an incredible organization that is fighting hard to protect LGBTQ rights in America. Donate here to join the fight! https://tilt.fyi/1sUy6Z2FeR

for more information about how the ACLU is working to protect LGBTQ rights in America, click here: https://www.aclu.org/issues/lgbtq-rights

This year we are raffling off 13 incredible prizes! Purchase raffle tickets by donating here https://tilt.fyi/1sUy6Z2FeR
