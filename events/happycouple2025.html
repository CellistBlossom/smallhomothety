Happy Couple Fundraiser 2025

The speedrunning community's fifth annual pride month fundraiser raised money for the ACLU. Various members of the community volunteered their time to do charity streams all month long, using a single donation link and stream layout to track donations. In total we raised $14,227, exceeding the $10,000 goal. Thanks to everyone who donated!!!

As a bonus, donors would enter a raffle to win various prizes made by speedrunning community members. A selection of planned streams for the fundraiser is listed below:
