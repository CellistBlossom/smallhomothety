Hollow Knight 8th Anniversary Celebration

HK8Y was the Hollow Knight 8th anniversary event! The event was spread out over the course of three days: main day, meme day, and rando day. Rando day was on Thursday, February 20th, 2025 starting at 5pm UTC (12pm EST).
