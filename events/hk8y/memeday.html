Hollow Knight 8th Anniversary Celebration

HK8Y is the Hollow Knight 8th anniversary event! The event was spread out over the course of three days: main day, meme day, and rando day. Meme day was on Friday, February 21th, 2025 starting at 5pm UTC (12pm EST).

Meme day is all about Hollow Knight category extensions! These categories are more "just for fun" than categories on the main board and include ones with more arbitrary goals. Yippee!
