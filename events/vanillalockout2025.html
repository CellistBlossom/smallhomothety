Vanilla Hand/Brain Lockout Tournament 2025

This tournament, sometimes known as "vanilla bingy tournament 3", was a double-elimination lockout tournament that ran from June 24th to September 29th, 2025.

Lockout is a bingo format played on a 5x5 bingo board. When one person marks a goal, the other player is "locked out" of it and can no longer mark it. For vanilla bingo, the first player to reach 13 goals or obtain 5 goals on the same row, column, or diagonal wins!

The tournament began with a two-week period of matches that were used to determine seeding. It was followed by a double-elimination bracket phase, which was restreamed on the HollowKnightCommunity Twitch channel and is now archived on Youtube.
