All Majors Randomizer Tournament May 2025

This tournament is a one-day tournament taking place on May 10th, 2025. The tournament consists of three rounds: a qualifier, semi-final, and final round. All three rounds happen concurrently, and all teams play the same seed per round. The first 6 teams to finish in the qualifiers move onto the semi-finals, with the rest eliminated from the tournament. The top two teams from the semi-finals get to battle it out in the third and final round!

All Majors is a semi-spoiler format. Each team has a counter of major items remaining in each area, but they don't know what the items are or their exact locations. Their goal is to find all of these major items and finish with both players defeating the Radiance! In this tournament, the items considered "major items" are:

Gimmicks

"Gimmicks" are extra settings added to the base tourney settings to spice up the match. This tournament will be played without gimmicks for the first two rounds, while the final round is required to have two gimmicks.
