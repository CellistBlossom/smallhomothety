Miner Timesaves Arcadian Tournament

"Miner Timesaves" was a latest patch Myla% tournament held on Jaunary 29th, 2022. The goal of Myla% is to kill Myla as quickly as possible, which also requires the runner to first infect her by obtaining Crystal Heart.

An arcadian tournament is one where top runners are barred from playing, giving newer runners the chance to play more matches and improve their skills. In this case, any runner with a Myla% time within 7% of world record (19:04 at the time), as well as any runner with a top 3 time in any Hollow Knight category with at least 10 runners, was barred from playing.

The tournament was single-elimination, and all matches were best of one. An additional match was held towards the end of the tournament to break the tie between third and fourth place.
