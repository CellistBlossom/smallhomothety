Path of Pain% Tournament 2021

Path of Pain% is a meme category where players must complete Path of Pain from a fresh file, starting in King's Pass. This differs from the individual level run, where players are given full movement and are only required to complete Path of Pain.

This tournament was a double-elimination tournament that ran from July 17th to October 2nd, 2021. It was one of the first events that was restreamed on the HollowKnightCommunity Twitch channel!
