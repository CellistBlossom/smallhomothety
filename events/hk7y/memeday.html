Hollow Knight 7th Anniversary Celebration: Meme Day

The Hollow Knight 7th anniversary is fast approaching! This year, the event will be broken into three parts: the main event, meme day, and rando day. Meme day will begin on Friday Feb 23rd at 8:00pm UTC, and the main event will begin on Saturday Feb 24th at 2:00pm UTC.

"Meme Day" means that this day is all about speedrunning category extensions! These are categories that are more "just for fun" and are usually taken less seriously than one on the main board such as any%. They usually have more arbitrary end-goals as well. See the schedule below for some fun examples of what will be in the event! Please keep in mind that this schedule is subject to change.
