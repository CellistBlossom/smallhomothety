Hollow Knight 7th Anniversary Celebration: Meme Day

HK7Y is over! Thank you so much to our runners, our commentators, our restreamers, and of course, our wonderful event organizers for making this event possible. If you missed the event, you can still watch it on our community Twitch channel!

HK7Y was the Hollow Knight 7th anniversary event which took place in February 2024. The event was broken into three parts: rando day, which took place on Thursday Feb 22nd at 8:00pm UTC; meme day, which took place on Friday Feb 23rd at 8:00pm UTC; and the main event, which took place on Saturday Feb 24th at 2:00pm UTC.

Watch

"Meme Day" means that this day was all about speedrunning category extensions! These are categories that are more "just for fun" and are usually taken less seriously than one on the main board such as any%. They usually have more arbitrary end-goals as well. See the schedule below for a list of the fun categories that were showcased in this event! Credit to dragon_nyx for the cool png version!
