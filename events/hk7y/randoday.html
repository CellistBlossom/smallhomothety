Hollow Knight 7th Anniversary Celebration: Rando Day

The Hollow Knight 7th anniversary is fast approaching! This year, the event will be broken into three parts: the main event, meme day, and rando day. Rando day will begin on Thursday Feb 22nd at 8:00pm UTC, meme day will begin on Friday Feb 23rd at 8:00pm UTC, and the main event will begin on Saturday Feb 24th at 2:00pm UTC.

Rando day means that this day is all about the Randomizer mod for Hollow Knight! There are dozens of ways to enjoy randomizer, and we'll be showcasing some of our favorites. For more information on randomizer, see the extensive randomizer section on this website!

The schedule for the rando day event is below. Please keep in mind that this schedule is subject to change.
