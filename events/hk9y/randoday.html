Hollow Knight 9th Anniversary Celebration

HK9Y is the Hollow Knight 9th anniversary event! The event will be spread out over the course of four days: main day, meme day, rando day, and bingy day. Rando day will take place on February 19th, 2026 starting at 3:00pm UTC (10:00am EST).
