Hollow Knight 9th Anniversary Celebration Meme Day

HK9Y is the Hollow Knight 9th anniversary event! The event will be spread out over the course of four days: main day, meme day, rando day, and bingy day. Meme day will take place on February 20th, 2026 starting at 6:00pm UTC (1:00pm EST).
