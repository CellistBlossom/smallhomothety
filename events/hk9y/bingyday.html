Hollow Knight 9th Anniversary Celebration

HK9Y is the Hollow Knight 9th anniversary event! The event was spread out over the course of four days: main day, meme day, rando day, and bingy day. Bingy day was on February 18th, 2026 starting at 2:00pm UTC (9:00am EST).

The schedule for the main event is below. Credit to dragon_nyx for the cool png version!
