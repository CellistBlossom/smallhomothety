The tournament will consist of three rounds: a qualifier, a top cut round, and a final round. The first 40% (subject to change) teams from the first round will move on to the second, and only the top two teams from the second round will play in the finals.

The tournament is played 2v2, but there's a catch: runners are not allowed to use the Itemsync mod to sync progression between them and their teammate!
