Grub-a-Thon 2025

We're bringing back Grub-a-Thon for 2025! The event will take place on June 7th, 2025. As part of the speedrunning community's annual Happy Couple fundraiser, we'll be raising money for the ACLU, a charity dedicated to protecting the rights of minorities, including those of the LGBTQ+ community.

As for the runs included, Grub-a-Thon is all about saving grubs! We'll be running a bunch of grub-themed speedrunning categories and randomizer formats. Yippee grubbies!!!!!
