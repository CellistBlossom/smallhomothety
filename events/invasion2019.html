Randomizer Invasion Tournament 2019

For more information on randomizer tournaments like this one, see the randomizer tournaments page.


This was a 1v1 invasion lockout tournament for the Randomizer2 mod hosted on the old community Twitch channel (HollowKnightSpeedruns). This tournament began with a group stage followed by a double elimination stage.

Invasion is a lockout format where players must first claim a side of the bingo board, and then progress from that side to "invade" their opponent's side. For a visual guide, see this imgur post: Invasion bingo rules
