Hollow Knight 9th Anniversary Celebration

HK9Y is the Hollow Knight 9th anniversary event! The event was spread out over the course of four days: main day, meme day, rando day, and bingy day. The main event was on February 21st, 2026 starting at 2:00pm UTC (9:00am EST).
