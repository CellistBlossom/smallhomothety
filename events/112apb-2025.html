112% All Pantheon Bosses Tournament 2025

This tournament, also known as the "Scarlet Cup", was a double-elimination speedrunning tournament for the 112% All Pantheon Bosses No Major Glitches category. The tournament was hosted with English commentary on the HollowKnightCommunity Twitch channel with a Chinese restream on Bilibili. The tournament ran from July 7th to August 6th 2025.

Seeding for the tournament was determined by the runners' 112% PBs on June 23rd, 2025. There was a $980 prize pool to be split among the winners of the tournament.
