Vanilla 3-lines Tournament 2025

This tournament was a short vanilla bingo tournament with the goal of getting 3 lines on a bingo board. It ran from January 25th to 26th, 2025.

The tournament consisted of five rounds in which all players raced simultaneously, with the slowest members being eliminated each round until only one was left. There was a $200 prize pool for this tournament. This tournament was not restreamed, though it did require video evidence, so some runners streamed on their personal channels.
