All Skills Tournament 2021

This tournament was broken into two separate events: one for runners playing on patch 1.2.2.1, and one for runners playing on the latest patch. The tournament ran from February 15th to July 24th, 2021.

The 1.2.2.1 event began with 5 rounds of Swiss. The top 16 players in the Swiss phase advanced to a double-elimination bracket. Because the latest patch event had fewer than sixteen entrants, it only consisted of a double-elimination phase.

The tournament was hosted on Speedgaming.
