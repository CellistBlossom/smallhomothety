Silksong Any% Tournament 2025

This was a tournament for the Any% No Major Glitches category that ran from Novermber 1st, 2025 to March 1st, 2026. The tournament is alternatively known as "Taiga's Silksong Any% Tournament".

The tournament consisted of an upper and lower bracket. The upper bracket contained the top 32 runners by PB, with the remainder in the lower bracket. Each tournament started out with best-of-one single-elimination, and the top 8 players from that phase progressed to a best-of-three double-elimination bracket. There were no patch restrictions for this tournament.
