About

Once upon a time, we lived in a world where tons of information was available online through personal and/or public websites. These days, message boards are dying, everyone is on TikTok, and you have to join a Discord to find out anything meaningful about speedrunning. Small Homothety Organization is dedicated to fixing that, by providing static links to well-organized pages on topics of my choosing!

So, hi, I'm Blossom! This is mostly an infodump site about my interests, but I'm open to suggestions. Anyone can contribute or suggest things by DMing me on Discord, emailing me, or submitting something to the suggestion box.
