Elegy% Random Grub Order

Elegy% is a category where you free all of the grubs in Hollow Knight in order to receive Grubberfly's Elegy from the Grubfather. Elegy% Random Grub Order puts a twist on the category by making you save all of the grubs in a random order! This is a fun way to play the category both by yourself and as a race with friends!
