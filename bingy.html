BINGY!

What is Bingy? Bingy is the commonly-used term to refer to Vanilla Hollow Knight Bingo (Vanilla meaning non-randomized, not unmodded). Bingo is played on a 5-by-5 board of squares each containing a goal, which is to be completed within the Hollow Knight game. Typically, the goal of bingy is to complete an objective as fast as possible. The specific objective depends on the format - read more about formats here. Those new to bingy should check out the Getting Started page!
