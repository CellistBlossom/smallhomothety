The amount of things to learn as a new glitched runner can seem intimidating, so I've compiled a list of categories as recommendations for where to start! You should also check out my playlist of glitch tutorials if you need help, and my playlist of meme runs for routes, and don't forget to join the Discord if you haven't already.

Note that while I recommend that you run these on current patch, and have made this guide with that in mind, there are lots of good 1028 categories to try! I won't cover them here, but you can ask in the Discord about anything you want to know about.
