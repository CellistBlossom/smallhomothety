Bingy Goals

This is the full list of goals contained in the Tournament 3, or Tourney 3, goalpack, which is the goalpack predominantly used for most bingy games & formats.

Failable Goals

Some goals you can lock yourself out of, meaning you'll have to create an entire new save file to go back and mark that goal (or in some cases, spend a lot of time doing many unwanted extra steps) if you still want or need to mark it. Be wary! Goals you can lock yourself out of are:

  • Defeat Colosseum Zote: If you skip VFK in greenpath, Zote will die and be unable to appear in the Colosseum, and you will be locked out of the goal for that save file.
  • Fragile Heart, Greed, and Strength: If you complete Give Divine a Charm before collecting all three fragile charms, the goal will not mark. You will need to purchase the charm you gave to Divine for the unbreakable version, or banish the Grimm Troupe, to get it back and mark the goal.
  • Have 2 Pale Ore: If you get Nail 2 before you collect your second pale ore, you will need to collect an additional pale ore, as you must have 2 in your inventory for this goal to mark.
  • Have 5 Hallownest Seals: If you sell to Lemm before getting your fifth seal, you will need to collect additional seals to get to 5, as you must have 5 in your inventory for this goal to mark.
  • Have 5 Wanderer's Journals: If you sell to Lemm before getting your fifth seal, you will need to collect additional journals to get to 5, as you must have 5 in your inventory for this goal to mark.
  • Rescue Zote in Deepnest: If you skip VFK in greenpath, Zote will die and be unable to appear in Deepnest, and you will be locked out of the goal for that save file.
  • Talk to Hornet at CoT Statue + Herrah: If you do not talk to Hornet at the statue in City of Tears before defeating Hornet 2, or if you dream nail Herrah before defeating Hornet 2, she will no longer show up and you will be locked out of the goal for that save file.
  • Vengefly King + Massive Moss Charger: If you skip VFK in greenpath and then collect claw, you will need to obtain dive, dream nail, and a simple key to go to Godhome to kill VFK there.
  • Watch Cloth Die: If you do not rescue Cloth in Ancient Basin before fighting Traitor Lord, she will not appear in the fight and will not die, and you will be locked out of the goal for that save file.
  • Dream Nail Poggy Thorax and Blue Child Joni: If you leave Joni's Repose after collecting Joni's Blessing, her ghost will not appear when re-entering the roon, and you will be locked out of the goal for that save file.
  • Slash Zote's corpse in Greenpath: If you kill VFK in greenpath, Zote will survive and his corpse will not appear in greenpath later, and you will be locked out of the goal for that save file.

Goal-Specific Rules

  • The 3 collector grubs count towards the "Save the 5 grubs in CoT" goal.
  • Warping immediately after killing the Sanctum-entrance Soul Warrior and killing it again to mark the "Kill 2 Soul Warriors" goal is banned.
  • After putting 1500 geo in the bank, you may withdraw the geo.
