Because many people invest time in randomizer racing, the Hollow Knight Speedrunning Community occasionally hosts tournaments for racers to prove their prowess! These tournaments are usually organized in the discord server and restreamed on our community Twitch channel (though this hasn't always been the case).

Tournament races are generally held to be more competitive than non-tournament races. They generally consist of only one format and may have tournament-specific rules. Tournaments, if restreamed, are almost always streamed live.

For an explanation of individual formats that may be used in tournaments, see the formats page.

2023 All Majors Tournament

After a break for the holidays, Grand Finals is upon us! Kuroshi/aPress and Tim/Quatopine will battle it out on Saturday, January 13th 2024 at 8PM EST (1AM UTC). Grand finals will be a best of 5 with a one game advantage for the team in the upper bracket, so there will be multiple races that day between the two teams. Check out the HollowKnightCommunity Twitch channel to watch the commentated restream!


The tournament is a 2v2 All Major Items TE double-elimination tournament. All matches will be best of 1s until the end of the tournament, when we will switch to best of 3s for winners finals and losers finals. Grand finals will be a best of 5 with a one game advantage for the team in the upper bracket.

All Majors is a semi-spoiler format played with the MajorItemsByArea mod. Runners will have a tracker onscreen listing the number of major items in each area, but they don't know the exact locations of the items, or which major items were placed in what area. Each team is required to get every item on the tracker, and once the counter reaches zero, defeat the Radiance. The first team to have both players finish Radiance wins!

What counts as a Major Item?

For this tournament, the items that are required to be obtained are:

  • Mothwing Cloak (x2), Shade Cloak, Mantis Claw, Crystal Heart, Monarch Wings (x2), Isma's Tear, Swim
  • Dream Nail (x2), Dreamgate, Awoken Dream Nail, Lurien, Monomon, Herrah, White Fragment, Kingsoul, Void Heart
  • Vengeful Spirit (x2), Shade Soul, Desolate Dive (x2), Descending Dark, Howling Wraiths (x2), Abyss Shriek
  • Nail Upgrade (x4), Dash Slash, Cyclone Slash, Great Slash
  • King's Brand, Lumafly Lantern, Tram Pass, Elevator Pass, City Crest
  • Shopkeeper's Key, Elegant Key, Love Key, Grimmchild, Defender's Crest, Spore Shroom

Introducing gimmicks!

What are gimmicks? Gimmicks are items that will be added to the randomized pool beyond the default settings to help spice up each match. The first match will be played without gimmicks, however, starting with winner's round 2 and loser's round 2, players must add at least one gimmick to the settings. We will be using a ban/pick system for this. A list of gimmicks can be found below:

  • Randomizing checks not in basic settings- grimmkin flames, whispering roots, lore tablets, randomizing focus
  • Settings that alter logic with skips- precise movement setting, background and enemy pogo, infection skips, no swim/no tear
  • Settings that alter how rando functions- ghost essence, lemm/junk shops, colo3 (the glory of being a fool mod)
  • +gimmicks voted upon by rando community- map area transition rando, 3 masks + 2 Charm notches, Split Claw, Fragile charms being major items

Note that some gimmicks not listed may be included if both teams and the Tournament Organizer agree to them, on a race-by-race basis. When in doubt, check the tracker on the restream!
