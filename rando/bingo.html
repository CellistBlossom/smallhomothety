Bingo boards for races!

Races using bingo boards are common in HKSC to add more depth to the race. Most bingo races, both randomizer and vanilla, are played using one of several Bingosync generators.

Most vanilla races use the "normal" generator, while randomizer races usually use the "Item Randomizer - No tiebreakers" for solo races, and the normal "Item Randomizer" board for team races. The DAB generator is usually used for Double Anti Bingo races. Most HKSC races use the password "eggu" for our races! For a list of randomizer formats, see here.

You can also create a custom bingo board by selecting "Custom (Advanced)" as your game instead of Hollow Knight. This should be pasted into the "Board" field as a JSON list of goals. Some members of the community have put together custom goal lists for this purpose. For example, here is the blackout generator we sometimes use!

A separate generator is used for some randomizer formats that can't be played on a traditional Bingosync board. It can be found here.
