Mods for Racing Rando!

Required/Highly Recommended mods for rando racing (note: some required mods are not on this list but will be downloaded as dependencies for other mods that are on the list):

  • AdditionalTimelines (displays some timelines for post-race screenshot sharing)
  • BingoUI (helps keep track of certain things for bingo races)
  • CompassAlwaysOn (allows player to track their location on in-game map even if Compass is not equipped)
  • ItemSync (allows items/checks obtained to be shared between teammates, used in team races)
  • MajorItemByAreaTracker (shows a list of all areas with how many "major items" each contain, used in All Majors races)
  • RandoMapMod (displays randomized item locations on the in-game map and allows player to view all maps in full, even if not obtained)
  • Randomizer 4
  • RandoPlus (adds other items to randomize, most notably nail upgrades)
  • RandoSettingsManager (allows rando to match ALL settings given a settings code)
  • RandoStats (displays skill timelines for post-race screenshot sharing)
  • RecentItemsDisplay (displays a list of recently obtained items)
  • Scatternest (allows multiple players in the same itemsync room to start at different locations)

(You should also get rainbow eggs and grub train. Just my personal opinion.)
