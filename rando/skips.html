Randomizer Skips!

Skips, though not always required for logic, allow the player to reach items and areas with a wider range of abilities than they would be able to without performing skips. There are many different types of skips, and new ones are being labbed all of the time! Skips can be required for logic by toggling them on in the settings menu.

A few resources for learning skips are listed below. If you want to learn more than is listed here, the #rando-resources channel in the Discord has many, many more!
